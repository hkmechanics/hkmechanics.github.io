Mechanical Art Studio

Mechanical motion drawn by hand, built like an instrument.

I create mechanical pieces where engineering and drawing meet: slow repeating motions, gentle rhythms, and small machines that feel alive.

Each work begins as a sketch of movement, then becomes a mechanism, a light, or a quiet object in space.
